# Vision - MilkDrop3 Omni-Workspace

## Long-Term Product Goal
The MilkDrop3 Omni-Workspace aims to be the definitive, autonomous management hub for the MilkDrop 3.0 ecosystem and its associated projects. It serves as a centralized "control plane" for a vast collection of submodules, including AI-driven operating systems, game engines, and web platforms, all orbiting the core MilkDrop3 visualization technology.

## Design Direction
- **Autonomy:** The workspace should be self-healing, self-documenting, and capable of orchestrating complex updates across dozens of submodules with minimal human intervention.
- **Integration:** Deep integration between the MilkDrop3 engine and AI orchestration layers (like Borg and AIOS) to create a reactive, intelligent visualization environment.
- **Portability:** Maintaining the "standalone" nature of MilkDrop3 while leveraging the power of a monorepo-like management structure.
- **AI-Native:** Designed from the ground up to be navigated and maintained by AI agents, with clear protocols and machine-readable documentation.
